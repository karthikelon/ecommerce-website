About Us

Artisanstore an initiative to provide a digital platform to the rural artisans and their crafts which have the potential to get worldwide recognition.

Our mission is to empathize with the skill of the rural artisan and aims to empower their craft and handmade product.

Our vision is to create an international community for rural artisans by promoting their skills, sharing diversity, and doing a cultural collaboration. We create a bridge between the potential customer and our artisan and help them to generate revenue through our digital platform.
